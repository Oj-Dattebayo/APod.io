Welcome to Celestial Showcase Web Development with NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day

The APOD (Astronomy Picture of the Day) is a NASA service that offers a daily image of snapshot of our universe along with a brief explanation given by an expert astronomer.Every day since it began on June 16, 1995, a new image has appeared on it. While APOD strives to post a new image every day, there have occasionally been reprints or cases in which an image from a previous date may have been used.Additionally, the picture for the current day might not yet be accessible depending on when you request the API.
